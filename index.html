Meet Angelica, She discovered her passion for web development and graphic design at Texas State University. She has built websites, created graphics, and designed advertising for a wide range of clients.
She is proud to use her technical and creative talents to help small businesses thrive. Angelica works alongside members of the coworking space, Geekdom, in the heart of downtown San Antonio.
